Ingredients:
 - 1 can of spaghetti sauce
 - 1 onion chopped
 - 1 package of mushrooms
 - 1 pound of ground beef
 - 1 package of spaghetti noodles

Directions:
 1. Cook onion until translucent
 2. Add ground beef, brown and drain fat
 3. Add sauce and mushrooms
 4. Heat up salted water
 5. Add noodles to water for 10 minutes
 6. Serve noodles with sauce
